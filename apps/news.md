Privacy Policy
Last Updated: May 08, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our handles user data in relation to our Facebook Application, which is used solely to facilitate social interactions (Like, Share, and Comment) via embedded posts.

1. Information We Collect
Our application is designed to be "read-only" and "interaction-only" via Facebook’s official SDKs/Plugins.

Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personal identification information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers on our private servers.

Facebook Data: When you use the Like, Share, or Comment buttons, the data is processed directly by Facebook. Our app does not "capture" the content of your comments or your profile details for our own databases.

2. How We Use Information
The integration serves only the following purposes:

To allow users to Like or Share content directly from our interface to their Facebook profile.

To display the Comment section of a public Facebook post for user engagement.

3. Data Storage and Retention
No Local Storage: Since we do not collect user data, we do not have a database containing your Facebook information.

Third-Party Processing: All interactions happen via Facebook's servers. Please refer to Facebook’s Data Policy for information on how they retain your interaction history.

4. Cookies and Tracking
Facebook may use cookies, web beacons, and other storage technologies to collect or receive information from our app/website to provide measurement services and target ads. You can opt-out of this via your Facebook account settings.

5. User Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
We respect your privacy rights. Since we do not store your data:

Access/Deletion: If you wish to delete a comment or "unlike" a post, you can do so directly on the Facebook platform.

Data Portability: Requests for data exports should be directed to Facebook, as they are the primary controller of the data generated by social plugins.

6. Data Deletion Instructions
To remove the app's access to your Facebook account:

Go to your Facebook Settings & Privacy. Click Settings.

Look for Apps and Websites.

Find ek and click Remove.
Note: Since we do not store your data on our servers, removing the app effectively severs all ties and no further data "deletion" is required on our end.
