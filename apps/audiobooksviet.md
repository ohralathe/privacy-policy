# Privacy Policy for AudiobooksViet App

This Privacy Policy is established by **ohralathe** for the mobile application **AudiobooksViet** (the "App").

## 1. Data Collection

**AudiobooksViet** is committed to protecting user privacy.

* **No Collection of Personally Identifiable Information (PII):** The **AudiobooksViet** App currently **does not collect** any personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email address, phone number) from its users.
* **No Collection of Device or Location Data:** The App does not collect device IDs, location information, or any other sensitive data.
* **App Data:** Usage state data (such as listening history and bookmarks) is stored **locally on your device** and is not transmitted externally. This data is removed when you uninstall the application.

The **AudiobooksViet** App currently **does not integrate** any analytics, advertising, or third-party services that collect user data.

## 3. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no risk of personal data leakage from the developer's side.
